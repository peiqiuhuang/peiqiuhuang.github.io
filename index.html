Biography

I am currently a Postdoc with the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Prof. Qingfu Zhang. I received my B.S. and M.S. degrees in College of Information Science and Engineering from Northeastern University in 2014 and 2017, respectively, and the Ph.D. degree in School of Automation from Central South University in 2021 under the supervision of Prof. Yong Wang.

Research Interest

My research interests include:

  • Computational Intelligence

  • Bilevel Optimization

  • Resource Management for Wireless Networks

Code & Datasets

VRPenstein: A highly-flexible and high-performance meta-heuristic with considerable large configuration space for the vehicle routing problems (VRP).

GA-EAX-restart: A restart version of the powerful Genetic Algorithm with Edge Assembly Crossover (GA-EAX by Nagata & Kobayashi). In our massive experiments, GA-EAX-restart could consistently outperform GA-EAX and LKH 2.0.9, the two state-of-the-art inexact solvers for TSP.
